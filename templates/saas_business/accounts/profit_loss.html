GST collected and paid is a pass-through liability to the government, not business income or expense — shown here for reference only and excluded from every profit figure above.

Note on Opening/Closing Stock: the app doesn't keep a day-by-day stock ledger, so Closing Stock is today's current inventory valuation (quantity × cost price, same method as the Inventory report), and Opening Stock is derived from it using the standard identity (COGS = Opening Stock + Purchases − Closing Stock) rather than a true historical snapshot. Accurate for the current period; an approximation for past ones.
