About BizManager

Business management software, built for Indian shop owners.

BizManager is a business management SaaS platform for small and medium retail businesses in India. It replaces the mix of notebooks, spreadsheets, and disconnected apps that many shop owners rely on with one system that handles billing, inventory, customers, suppliers, purchases, expenses, and GST-ready reporting.

What BizManager does

  • Billing & POS — fast point-of-sale invoicing for walk-in and repeat customers.
  • Inventory — stock tracking with low-stock alerts across product categories.
  • Customers & Suppliers — receivables, payables, and purchase history in one place.
  • GST & Accounts — GST-ready invoices and a double-entry accounting engine underneath every transaction.
  • Reports — day-to-day and month-to-month visibility into how the business is doing.

Who it's for

BizManager is built specifically around how Indian shop owners work: GST compliance, lakh/crore currency formatting, and workflows for multi-person teams (owner, manager, accountant roles) inside a single shop account.

How sign-in works

Business owners and staff sign in with their registered mobile number and a 6-digit PIN. Platform administrators use a separate, invitation-only administrator login with a password and one-time code. BizManager never asks for your password or PIN by email, SMS, or phone call — only through the sign-in page on this site.
