Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ policy_updated }}

This policy explains what information BizManager collects when you sign up for and use the platform, and how that information is used, stored, and protected.

Information we collect

  • Account information — full name, mobile number, email address, and a hashed PIN or password. We never store passwords or PINs in plain text.
  • Business data — the products, customers, suppliers, invoices, and transactions you enter to run your business.
  • Usage & security data — login timestamps, IP address, and audit logs of key account actions, used to protect your account and diagnose issues.

How we use it

  • To provide and operate your BizManager account and the business modules you use (billing, inventory, GST, accounts, and reports).
  • To send OTPs, login alerts, and service notifications by email or SMS.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your business data or customer lists to third parties.
  • We do not ask for your password, PIN, or OTP over email, phone, or SMS — only through the official BizManager sign-in page.

Data storage & security

Data is stored in an access-controlled database and transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. Access to a business's data is scoped to that business's own account and team members.

Your choices

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account by contacting us — see the Contact page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
